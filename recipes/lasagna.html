Lasagna

Description

Look no farther. Here is the best lasagna recipe you'll ever want. Originating from the minds of Italian chefs Giuliano Bugialli and Elodia Rigante, this recipe is positively Italian. The sauce is intensely favorful, and the cheese melts into creaminess that will delight you with each fork-full.

But this lasagna is not for the faint of heart. It will take close to four hours to make but it will be well worth it!

Ingredients

For The Sauce

  • 1 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 medium red onions, finely diced
  • 2 large cloves of minced garlic
  • 8 ounces pancetta, diced
  • A dash of salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 11/2 cups good red wine, preferably Italian
  • 2 28-ounce cans Italian plum tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 3/4 pound ground sirloin
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated pecorino Romano
  • 2 eggs
  • 10 sprigs fresh parsley, leaves only, washed and dried
  • 2 large whole cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 pound Italian sausage, a mix of hot and sweet

For The Lasagna

  • 1 15-ounce container ricotta cheese
  • 2 extra-large eggs
  • 2 cups freshly grated pecorino Romano
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  • 1 pound mozzarella, grated
  • 16 sheets fresh lasagna noodles, preferably Antica Pasteria
