Homemade French Bread

Description

This homemade French bread is crusty on the outside and chewy on the inside, and is one of the best things to come out of the oven.  This recipe is easier to make than you might think. Try it out and you will never want to go back to those mediocre loaves from the store.

Ingredients

  • 2 ¼ cups warm water around 110 degrees F
  • 1 ½ Tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon instant or active dry yeast
  • 2 Tablespoons vegetable oil (olive oil or canola oil also works
  • 2 teaspoons table salt
  • 5 ½-6 cups all-purpose or bread flour, scooped & leveled (776 to 846g)
  • 1 large egg beaten with 1 teaspoon cold water
  • Melted salted butter for brushing on top of the baked loaves (optional)
